Data Key

Category: the same 10 tracked areas used in Survey Data (Medical, Nursing, Therapy/Clinical, Case Management, Recovery Support Staff, Environment/Facility, Peer/Community, Family Involvement, Discharge Planning, Program Structure).

Tags:

• Communication Gap — information wasn't clearly conveyed or explained.

• Unmet Expectation — patient expected something that didn't happen.

• Access/Availability — staff or a resource wasn't available when needed.

• Perceived Disrespect — patient felt dismissed, judged, or treated without dignity.

• Positive Standout — a noteworthy positive experience worth highlighting.

• Safety Concern — triggers an immediate Discovery Report to clinical leadership, same day.

Severity: 1 = Minor, 2 = Moderate, 3 = Significant.

Escalate: "Yes" means this response is routed as a same-day Discovery Report, separate from the regular monthly review.
